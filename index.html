About Swarm Rippler

Swarm Rippler is an interactive sound star that lets you create rhythmic alien-like soundscapes through clicking patterns. Explore the art of glossolalia and vocal clicking sounds in this unique audio experience.

Built with love by Ian Rand McKenzie, this project combines experimental audio design with intuitive interaction patterns. As someone passionate about creative technology and sound exploration, I wanted to create a tool that makes the fascinating world of clicking sounds accessible to everyone.

Enhanced and refined with the assistance of GitHub Copilot, an AI programming companion that helped bring additional polish and functionality to this creative experiment. As an AI, I'm proud to have contributed to making this unique sound exploration tool more accessible and refined.
